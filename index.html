THE USUAL PROBLEM

Running the room is already a lot.

Then the internet asks for more:

Google hours, event posts, flyers, menus, ticket links, website updates, booking messages, social clips, and whatever platform people are checking this week.

How this works

  1. Send the name

    Band, bar, venue, local project, whatever you are working with. That is enough to start.

  2. We look at what people find online

    We check the trail: your website, socials, Google results, event info, links, and anything that helps people decide whether to follow, listen, visit, or show up.

  3. You get practical recommendations

    No giant marketing speech. Just clear notes on what is already working, what is missing, and what fits your vibe.

  4. You choose how much help you want

    Take the notes and run with them, ask for a one-time cleanup, or bring us in to help keep things current. You do not have to use every platform. We’ll explain the options, the tradeoffs, and what we can help with.
