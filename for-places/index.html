THE USUAL PROBLEM

Running the room is already a lot.

People are checking before they show up. Some are looking for your place by name. Others are just looking for something to do tonight.

They might land on Google, Instagram, Facebook, an old flyer, a menu photo, a ticket link, or whatever post the group chat sends around.

If the details are scattered or stale, they may never get a clear answer: what's happening, when it starts, what the room feels like, or why they should come through.

We help make the online trail clearer, more current, and easier to keep updated.

Setup

Make it easy for people to find you and understand what's happening.

We take a look at what you already have and clean it up so your online setup works the way it should. The goal is simple: someone new should be able to find your place, understand your nights, and know what to do next without guessing.

Includes

  • Review of your Instagram, Facebook, Google Business Profile, website, link pages, and other public-facing pages
  • Fixing incorrect hours, broken links, missing info, or confusing details
  • Clear weekly schedule setup so people know what happens each night
  • Bio and profile cleanup so people understand what you are, what you do, and what nights matter
  • Basic organization like a link hub, simple mobile-friendly page, or clearer navigation
  • Google Business Profile cleanup and optimization
  • Up to 10 QR table or bar inserts, sized 3x5 or 4x6
  • Clear recommendations on what to fix next

Best for

Bars and venues that feel scattered, outdated, hard to find, or unclear about what's happening week to week.
