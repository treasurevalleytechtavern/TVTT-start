How this works

  1. Choose where you need backup

    Pick the option that sounds closest: cleanup, a better home base, event support, content help, or ongoing digital support. You do not need to know the perfect package before reaching out. The form just helps us start in the right place.

  2. Send what you have

    Share your bar or venue name, website, socials, Google profile, menu, event calendar, flyers, videos, or whatever exists right now. Messy is fine. Half-updated is fine. "We meant to fix this months ago" is extremely normal.

  3. We check the online trail

    We look at what people actually find: your website or link page, Google results, socials, hours, menus, event info, booking info, photos, videos, and anything that helps someone decide whether to visit, follow, book, or show up.

  4. We build around the room

    You get practical recommendations and options that fit your actual place, not a generic marketing template. Maybe that is a cleaner link page. Maybe it is better event info. Maybe it is Google cleanup, live clips, menu updates, social help, or ongoing support to keep things current.

Setup

Make it easy for people to find you and understand what's happening.

We take a look at what you already have and clean it up so your online setup works the way it should. The goal is simple: someone new should be able to find your place, understand your nights, and know what to do next without guessing.

Includes

  • Review of your Instagram, Facebook, Google Business Profile, website, link pages, and other public-facing pages
  • Fixing incorrect hours, broken links, missing info, or confusing details
  • Clear weekly schedule setup so people know what happens each night
  • Bio and profile cleanup so people understand what you are, what you do, and what nights matter
  • Basic organization like a link hub, simple mobile-friendly page, or clearer navigation
  • Google Business Profile cleanup and optimization
  • Up to 10 QR table or bar inserts, sized 3x5 or 4x6
  • Clear recommendations on what to fix next

Best for

Bars and venues that feel scattered, outdated, hard to find, or unclear about what's happening week to week.

In The Room

Show people what it actually feels like to be there.

We show up and capture real moments as they happen, then give you usable content that reflects your bar or venue the way it actually is. Not staged. Not overproduced. Just proof that the room is alive.

Includes

  • On-site content capture through short visits, not full-night coverage
  • Footage of the crowd, room, bartenders, events, performers, regulars, and atmosphere
  • Short-form video clips ready for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and more
  • Light editing to keep clips clean and usable
  • Organized delivery so you can post the content yourself
  • Content that reflects real energy, not fake promo gloss

Best for

Bars and venues that want real content from the room but do not have the time, staff, or brain-space to capture it themselves.
