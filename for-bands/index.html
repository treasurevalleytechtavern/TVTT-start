THE USUAL PROBLEM

Doing band shit is already a full-time job. Between rehearsal schedules, writing riffs, loading heavy gear, and managing everyone's different levels of group-chat awareness, you do not have time to act as your own digital marketing department.

When your online setup gets scattered, local bookers, venues, and potential fans hit an immediate wall of friction:

  • The Buried Show Trailer

    Your next big date or flyer is trapped across random posts, text threads, and dead grids instead of sitting front and center.

  • The Broken Link Penalty

    A booking agent clicks your info link to check out your music, hits an unverified or dead page, and instantly moves to the next band.

  • The Lost Camera Roll Clips

    The highest-energy video footage from your last packed house sits forgotten on a phone screen instead of proving you can draw a room.

  • The Blind Search Friction

    People hear your name, look you up on their phones, and still cannot figure out where to listen, when to show up, or how to buy merch.

If your online footprint looks like an afterthought, venues and booking agents will treat you like one.

A customized digital home base built to automate a high-visibility tour run.

When Pacific Northwest heavy music outfit Oathbound launched their MySpace-themed tour, a generic link list would have completely killed the aesthetic momentum. They needed an online landing zone that matched the nostalgic world of the tour while handling real-time logistical infrastructure.

  • Aesthetic Integration: A temporary, custom-coded tour portal styled to match the specific era and branding of the run.
  • Automated Calendar Logic: System programming that automatically shifts upcoming dates, venue details, and age restrictions into a past-show archive the minute the doors close, meaning zero website maintenance while the band is on the highway.
  • Frictionless Routing: Centralized ticket and venue landing gates mapped perfectly for immediate mobile checkout.

The result: a reliable, zero-maintenance home base that handles the administrative work while the lineup handles the floor.
